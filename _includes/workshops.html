AKBC 2019 will feature a few thematic workshops where you can deep dive into a particular topic and connect with other researchers working in the same sub-area. However, rather than deciding on the topics ourseleves, we are planning to open up the topic selection process and let the community decide based on discussions and feedback. Please visit discuss.akbc.ws to join the discussion!

Current topics include:

  • Neural and Symbolic Representation and Reasoning
  • Scientific Literature Knowledge Bases
  • Knowledge Bases and Multiple Modalities
  • More coming soon!

If you have any thoughts, please feel free to get with touch with Xiang (xiangren [at] usc.edu) or Partha (ppt [at] iisc.ac.in)
